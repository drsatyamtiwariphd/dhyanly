Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026 · Version 1.0
Core Commitment

Dhyanly™ is built on a principle of radical on-device privacy. No personal health data, screen content, or biometric information is ever transmitted off your device to our servers. All AI analysis happens locally. You own your data entirely.

1. Who We Are

Dhyanly™ is a product of YogaXBiofeedback Private Limited (CIN: U62011DL2026PTC462641), incorporated in New Delhi, India. This Privacy Policy applies to all Dhyanly™ applications including MCAS, DhyanlyApp, SvaraFlow, and YogaArena ("the Suite").

Contact: satyam@yogaxbiofeedback.com

2. What Data We Collect

The Dhyanly™ web suite in its current form collects no personal data whatsoever. Specifically:

  • No account creation or login is required
  • No names, email addresses, or contact information are collected
  • No health data, questionnaire responses, or wellness scores are stored on any server
  • No screen content, camera frames, or biometric readings are transmitted off-device
  • No cookies are set by this website beyond technical session requirements
  • No third-party analytics or advertising scripts are embedded
3. MCAS — Screen Analysis Data

The Mindful Content Awareness System (MCAS) performs on-device analysis of screen content. This means:

  • Screen frames are captured, analysed, and immediately deleted from memory — they are never saved to disk or transmitted
  • OCR tokens extracted from the screen are discarded after classification
  • Only aggregated session summaries (content category percentages, duration, risk scores) are stored locally in your browser's local storage
  • You can delete all MCAS data at any time via Settings → Clear All Data
4. DhyanlyApp — Health Questionnaire Data

Responses to GAD-2, PHQ-2, and PSS questionnaires are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to YogaXBiofeedback or any third party
  • Is deleted when you clear your browser data or use the in-app data deletion feature
5. YogaArena — Camera Data

YogaArena requests access to your device camera for real-time pose detection. Camera data is processed entirely on-device using the MediaPipe library. No video frames are recorded, stored, or transmitted at any point.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

This website uses browser local storage solely to preserve your in-app settings and session data between visits. No third-party cookies are used. No advertising cookies are used. You can clear all stored data via your browser settings at any time.

7. Third-Party Services

The Suite loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). This is the only external network request made by the website. Google's privacy policy governs this interaction. No other third-party scripts, trackers, or services are loaded.

8. Children's Privacy

Dhyanly™ is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used the service, please contact us immediately.

9. Your Rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete on our end. All data generated by your use of Dhyanly™ resides exclusively on your own device and is entirely under your control.

10. Research Mode

Certain apps offer an optional Research Mode for academic use. This is strictly opt-in, requires explicit informed consent, and exports only anonymised aggregate data with no personally identifiable information. Research Mode data exports are compliant with IRB (Institutional Review Board) requirements.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the Suite evolves. Material changes will be noted at the top of this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Suite after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at satyam@yogaxbiofeedback.com. We aim to respond within 48 hours.
