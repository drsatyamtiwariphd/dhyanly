The Mission
To make India's contemplative science measurable, accessible, and personalised — through rigorous research and technology that respects the depth of the tradition it draws from.
🔬
The Research
Our work is grounded in peer-reviewed RCTs, classical Sanskrit texts (Shiva Swarodaya), and the LNABA framework connecting nasal cycle physiology to autonomic neuroscience. IIT Mandi affiliated.
🛡️
The Principles
Privacy-first. On-device. Transparent. Non-judgmental. We build tools that respect both your inner life and your data — because how technology treats you shapes how you experience yourself.
