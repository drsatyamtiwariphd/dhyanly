About the Event

Where Ancient Practice
Meets Intelligent Science

The International Dialogue on AI, Digital Health and Integrative Practices is the first dedicated international platform bringing together researchers, clinicians, technologists and practitioners at the precise intersection of ancient knowledge systems, neuroscience, biofeedback, artificial intelligence and digital health.

Work at this intersection has no single dedicated home. Researchers fragment their output across yoga journals, digital health journals, neuroscience journals and engineering journals — none capturing the full scientific scope. NADI-2026 changes that.

Three days of rigorous intellectual exchange through expert dialogues, structured debates, panels and masterclasses — ending with a 48-hour hackathon where the best teams present live and win prizes on Day 3.

Ancient Systems as Empirical Science
Yoga, Ayurveda and Svara Shastra treated as structured empirical datasets subject to rigorous validation.
Physiological Intelligence
HRV, EEG, respiratory patterns and nasal airflow as information-rich signals for training the autonomic nervous system.
Intelligent Intervention Design
AI-prescribed protocols, computer vision correction and digital therapeutics built on peer-reviewed evidence.
From Lab to Life
Live working technology demonstrations — not prototypes — showing how this science enters daily clinical practice.
The Integrative Vision

Five Domains. One Conversation.

NADI-2026 is built on the premise that these five domains are not separate fields — they are one field artificially divided. The most important science happens at their intersection.

Neuroscience & Biofeedback
HRV, EEG, autonomic regulation, resonance frequency and clinical psychophysiology
AI & Digital Health
Machine learning, adaptive interventions, wearables, mHealth platforms and digital therapeutics
Ancient Knowledge Systems
Yoga, Ayurveda, Svara Shastra, Sanskrit epistemology treated as empirical science
Yoga & Mind-Body Sciences
Yoga therapy, contemplative science, pranayama, meditation and clinical movement
Integrative Medicine
Longevity, translational research, medical devices, clinical validation and health policy

Session Architecture

How We Design Learning

No passive lectures. Every format demands active thinking from speakers and audience alike.

ED
Expert Dialogue
Sharp pre-prepared questions to 3–4 domain experts. Each answers in 5 minutes. No slides. Pure thinking out loud. The most intellectually dense format in the program.
SD
Structured Debate
Two experts take opposing positions on genuinely contested questions. 15 minutes each, 20 minutes dialogue, 10 minutes Q&A. Only where real intellectual disagreement exists.
PP
Panel with Provocation
Moderator opens with a deliberately provocative statement. Four experts respond, agree, disagree and build. Generates the most surprising and memorable content.
MC
Masterclass
One expert teaches something practical. Audience participates simultaneously. 45 minutes. Participants leave with a skill — not just information.
LR
Live Research Unpacking
One researcher presents one unpublished finding. Audience asks the hardest questions. 30 minutes of science in progress.
TD
Technology Demonstration
Live demonstration of working intelligent systems. Real biofeedback data. Real AI correction. Real hardware. A scientific demonstration, not a product pitch.

Satyam Tiwari is a PhD Scholar at the Indian Knowledge Systems and Mental Health Applications, IKSMHA centre, IIT Mandi. His research focuses on contemplative neuroscience with a specific emphasis on Svara Yoga, nostril-specific breathing, heart rate variability and EEG.

He conducted the largest Svara Yoga HRV randomised controlled trial in the domain (n=150, 5-group design), developing the LNABA theoretical model — Lateralized Nasal Airflow, Brain and Autonomic function — integrating Svara Shastra with modern HRV and EEG evidence through 151 verified citations. He holds a Gold Medal MSc in Yoga from MDNIY Delhi and won the PragnyaTech Deep-Tech Challenge at the Adani Global Indology Conclave 2025.

As Founder and CEO of YogaXBiofeedback Pvt Ltd, trading as Dhyanly, he builds the hardware and AI platforms that bridge this research with clinical and consumer applications. His courses have reached practitioners in 110+ countries and he has produced 30+ episodes for Doordarshan and All India Radio.

SvaraTherapy

AI-driven digital therapeutic prescribing personalized lateralized nostril breathing protocols based on real-time HRV biofeedback. Pilot study targeting JMIR mHealth. Live demo at NADI-2026.

PranaFlow · NasaFlow

Validated nasal airflow biofeedback hardware. PranaFlow achieves 94%+ measurement accuracy. NasaFlow next-generation integrating with SvaraTherapy. Both demonstrated live at NADI-2026.

Daily Yoga Sadhna Logger

AI-powered longitudinal yoga practice tracking platform. Part of the Dhyanly technology ecosystem. Live demonstration at NADI-2026.
